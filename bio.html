Biography

Ever since I can remember, I have been a fan of computers and everything related.

I am studying Computing in University to further my knowledge and use this passion to put my mind towards something that I am interested in, and hopefully make a career out of a pastime.

My hobbies include playing computer games and fashion.

When I am not sitting behind a computer, I can usually be found in the University library, or in bed, asleep.
